Who we are

A truly Filipino company, helping Filipinos.

FoodAsia Group (FoodAsia Corporation) was established in 2004 and has grown to be a multi-brand, large-sized company that interests in Food Manufacturing, Retail, Food Service, and Franchising.

Our major brands include Bibingkinitan!, LAVA, Papas Potato Chips, WuffWich, BBQ Shack, and Magnum, all of which have made their mark in the local food scene and soon will be expanding regionally throughout Southeast Asia.

FoodAsia Group provides employment and career opportunities to over 400 full-time employees and is a proud partner of trade and industry to farmers of Philippine products, due to the Farm-To-Table approach of our brands.

Food Manufacturing

FoodAsia manufactures consumer food products distributed thru our company-owned stores, franchised stores, and retail partners. With two (2) manufacturing plants and facilities, we strongly focus on bakery items, baked-goods, rice-based goods, flour-based products, and meat processing. Our facilities are state-of-the-art using advanced and modern equipment is 100% GMP compliant.

Restaurant & Café Operations

As part of our supply and distribution chains, FoodAsia also own and operates restaurants and cafes wherein our manufactured food products are sold and distributed. FoodAsia has a total store network of over two hundred fifty (250) units located in major cities and towns thoughout the whole Philippines

Food Service

FoodAsia also has a Food Service Division wherein our manufactured products are sold to institutional accounts like hotels, restaurants, canteens, offices, and the like. FoodAsia also offers other food establishments integrated food solutions, customized food preparations, including Toll Manufacturing.
